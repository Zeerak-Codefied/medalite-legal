Delete MedaLite Account

MedaLite respects your privacy and your right to control your data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your MedaLite account and associated data, please email us at:

support@medalite.com

Use the subject line: Delete My Account
Include the email address associated with your MedaLite account.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Authentication data
  • User preferences and usage data
  • FCM tokens
  • Connected device information

Data Retention

Once verified, all personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Some anonymized data may be retained for legal or security purposes.
