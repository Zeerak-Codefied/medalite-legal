Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2025

Introduction

MedaLite is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our meditation application and RGB light glasses.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Email address and username
  • Account credentials
  • Device information and settings
  • Usage data and preferences
  • FCM tokens for push notifications

Technical Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Connected device information (RGB glasses)

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain services
  • Authenticate user accounts
  • Control and sync RGB light glasses
  • Send notifications and updates
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze app performance
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Firebase services. We apply industry-standard security practices; however, no transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data may only be shared:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request corrections
  • Request account deletion
  • Opt-out of notifications
  • Withdraw consent

Children’s Privacy

MedaLite does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected within the application.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app support section.
